# FiveM Server Ideas

## 1. Dynamic Weather & Time Control
Real-time weather transitions (fog → rain → clear) with `/weather` admin override that affects traction, visibility, and police response times.

## 2. Advanced Housing System
Full interior customization (furniture placement via ox_target), multi-room support, guest keys, alarm systems, and garage-synced vehicle storage.

## 3. Drug Manufacturing & Distribution
4-stage grow-op (plant → harvest → process → package), randomized police raids, territory-based distribution hubs, risk/reward scaling.

## 4. Court & Legal System
Live courtroom with judge, prosecutor, defense lawyer, jury polling, evidence submission, sentencing guidelines, bail hearings, and appeal system.

## 5. Advanced Banking & Finance
Wire transfers, direct deposit paychecks, credit scores, loan applications with interest, ATM robberies (skill-check), investment portfolios.

## 6. Fishing & Marine Activities
Trawler boats, rod fishing off piers/beaches, fish market prices that fluctuate, rare catches, illegal fish smuggling, underwater loot crates.

## 7. Hunting & Wilderness
Deer/boar/coyote hunting with tracking mechanics, skinning/cooking, trophy mounts for properties, hunting licenses, poacher penalties.

## 8. Custom Gang Turf Wars
Capture-the-zone territory system, gang influence tracking, income from controlled businesses, raid cooldowns, alliance mechanics, K/D tracking.

## 9. Scuba Diving & Underwater Content
Oxygen management, underwater cave exploration, shipwreck loot, shark encounters, underwater treasure maps, rebreather upgrades.

## 10. Food Truck & Restaurant Business
Purchaseable food trucks, menu creation, ingredient stock, customer satisfaction ratings, delivery orders, health inspections.

## 11. Casino & Gambling
Blackjack, poker, roulette, slot machines, horse betting, casino heist, player-run poker tables, anti-cheat hand tracking.

## 12. Police Evidence Locker
Chain-of-custody evidence tracking, forensic analysis mini-game, evidence locker at PD, trial submission, evidence tampering risk.

## 13. Taxi & Ride-Sharing
Meter-based fares, customer ratings, dispatch jobs, VIP service tier, airport queue system, shift bonuses.

## 14. Newspaper & Media
Player-written articles, photography submissions, broadcasting van, live news reports, ad revenue, public perception system.

## 15. Civilian Jobs Expansion
Bus driver (route + schedule), garbage collector (street-by-street), mail carrier (address delivery), tow truck (call-out system).

## 16. Street Racing Scene
Organized races (sprint, circuit, drag), betting pool, NOS upgrades, police intercept missions, reputation leaderboard, crew colors.

## 17. Emergency Alert System
Weather warnings (tornado/hurricane path), AMBER alerts, evacuation zones, emergency shelters, FEMA-style response coordination.

## 18. Pet System
Dog/cat ownership, feeding/walking, tricks training, K9 integration for police, pet stores, adoption center, lost pet alerts.

## 19. Crafting & Smelting
Ore mining → smelting → crafting, weapon mods, tool crafting, ammunition presses, salvage from wrecks, workbench upgrades.

## 20. Prison Life & Breakout
Full prison interior, inmate jobs, contraband smuggling, prison guard duties, breakout planning (tunnel/truck/diversion), manhunt on escape.
